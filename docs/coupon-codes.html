Coupon Code FAQ

Is there a promo code I can enter online?

Great Clips doesn't have online checkout, so there's no place to enter a promo code. You pay at the salon after your haircut, where you show your coupon to get the discount.

Do Great Clips coupon codes expire?

Yes, most coupons are valid for 14 days after you first access them. We show expiration dates on each coupon when available.

Can I use multiple coupon codes?

No, Great Clips allows only one coupon per person per visit. Pick the best available deal!

Do codes work at all Great Clips locations?

Some coupons are universal (work anywhere), while others are location-specific. We clearly label each coupon so you know where it's valid.
