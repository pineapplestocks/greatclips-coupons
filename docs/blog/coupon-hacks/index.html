7 Great Clips Coupon Hacks to Save More in 2026

Want to pay less than $6 for a haircut? These insider tips will help you maximize your savings at Great Clips every single time.

1. Always Check for Coupons Before You Go

This sounds obvious, but you'd be surprised how many people pay full price. Great Clips regularly offers coupons through Facebook ads, and we collect them all on our homepage. New coupons appear almost daily, so always check before heading to the salon.

2. Use Online Check-In to Save Time (Not Money, But Still Worth It)

Great Clips' Online Check-In lets you add your name to the wait list from your phone. While it won't save you money directly, it means less time waiting in the salon. Check in, then browse for coupons while you wait!

3. Compare Location-Specific vs. Universal Coupons

Not all coupons are created equal. Sometimes a coupon for a specific Great Clips location offers a better deal than the universal ones. On our site, we show both:

  • Universal coupons - Work at any Great Clips in the US
  • Location-specific coupons - Only work at certain salons but may have better prices

Check if there's a location-specific coupon for a Great Clips near you before using the universal one.

4. Visit Multiple Locations (They're Franchises)

Here's something most people don't know: Great Clips salons are independently owned franchises. This means different locations can have different promotions. If one Great Clips near you doesn't have a good coupon, check another location in your area.

5. Time Your Haircuts Right

Great Clips tends to release more coupons during:

  • Back-to-school season (August-September)
  • Holiday periods (November-December)
  • Summer (June-July)
  • After slow periods (January-February)

If you can be flexible with when you get your haircut, wait for these peak coupon periods.

6. The 14-Day Rule

Most Great Clips coupons are valid for 14 days after you click on them. This means you can "save" a coupon by accessing it, then use it any time within those two weeks. Don't access the coupon until you're ready to use it within that window.

7. Senior and Kids Discounts Stack Differently

Great Clips already offers lower prices for seniors (65+) and kids (under 10). In some cases, you might actually get a better deal with the standard coupon than the senior/kid price. Do the math:

  • Senior haircut: ~$15
  • Adult haircut with $5.99 coupon: $5.99

The coupon wins! Always compare before assuming the senior/kid price is better.

Bonus: What NOT to Do

A few things to avoid:

  • Don't try to use multiple coupons - It's one coupon per visit, period.
  • Don't use expired coupons - They won't work and it wastes everyone's time.
  • Don't forget to show the coupon BEFORE - Mention it when you check in, not after the haircut.
