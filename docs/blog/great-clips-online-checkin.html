Great Clips Online Check-In

Skip the wait — check in from your phone before you arrive

Updated: March 2026

📱 The Short Version

Download the Great Clips app, find your location, and tap Check In. You'll get a text when it's your turn. No more sitting in a waiting room — wait from home or your car.

Great Clips online check-in is one of the best features they offer — and most people don't use it. Instead of walking in and waiting 30+ minutes, you can join the queue from your phone and only show up when it's almost your turn. Here's exactly how to do it.

How to Check In Online (Step-by-Step)

1

Download the Great Clips App

Free on App Store and Google Play. Search "Great Clips" — it's the official blue scissors logo. You don't need to create an account to check in.

2

Find Your Nearest Location

The app shows nearby Great Clips with real-time wait times. Pick the one you want. You can see how many people are ahead of you before committing.

3

Tap "Check In"

Enter your name and mobile number. That's it — you're in the queue. The app shows your estimated wait time and your position in line.

4

Wait Anywhere — Then Head Over

Do whatever you want while you wait. The app (and a text message) will alert you when you're next. Aim to arrive 5 minutes before your turn.

No App? Check In on the Website

You can also check in without downloading the app:

  1. Go to greatclips.com on your phone browser
  2. Click "Find a Salon"
  3. Search your zip code and select your location
  4. Click "Check In Now" and enter your name + phone number

Pro Tips to Save Even More Time

✓

Check the wait before you commit. The app shows wait times without checking in. If one location has a 45-min wait but another nearby has 10 min, choose the shorter one.

✓

Have your coupon ready. Pull up your coupon from greatclipsdeal.com before you arrive. Show it to the stylist before the cut starts.

✓

Enable notifications. Allow push notifications from the Great Clips app so you don't miss your alert. The text message backup is also helpful.

✓

Don't check in too early. If you check in with a 30-min wait and then can't leave, you'll be stuck waiting at the salon anyway. Check in when you're actually ready to head out.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Great Clips online check-in free?

Yes, 100% free. The app is free to download and there's no charge to check in online.

Can I cancel my online check-in?

Yes. Open the app and cancel your check-in at any time before your appointment. Or just don't show up — there's no penalty.

Does Great Clips online check-in work the same day?

Yes — it's same-day only. Great Clips doesn't take advance bookings. Check in when you're ready to go.

Can I check in for multiple people?

You can check in one person per device. For a family, each member can check in on their own phone, or check in one at a time and walk in together.
