Frequently Asked Questions

Does Great Clips take the last customer right before closing?

Generally yes, if you're checked in before closing time. Use online check-in to secure your spot — once you're in the queue you'll be served.

Are Great Clips hours the same everywhere?

No. Hours vary by location, franchisee, and shopping center agreements. Always check your specific location.

Is Great Clips open on New Year's Eve?

Yes, most locations are open on New Year's Eve, often with slightly reduced hours (closing around 6-7pm).
