Frequently Asked Questions

Does Great Clips cut toddler hair?

Yes. Great Clips stylists are used to young children. Some locations have booster seats. Go during off-peak hours for a less stressful experience.

How long does a kids haircut take at Great Clips?

A kids haircut typically takes 10-15 minutes, plus any wait time. Use online check-in to minimize the wait.

Can I use one coupon for multiple kids?

No — Great Clips coupons are one per customer per visit. You'll need a separate coupon for each child. Each coupon links to a unique offer URL.

Does Great Clips cut girls' hair?

Yes, Great Clips cuts all hair types. They do basic cuts for girls. For more complex styles (layers, bobs, etc.), bring a reference photo.
