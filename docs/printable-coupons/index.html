How to Print Your Great Clips Coupon

  1. 1

    Find Your Coupon

    Visit our homepage and click on the coupon you want to use.

  2. 2

    Open the Official Coupon Page

    You'll be taken to the official Great Clips coupon page (offers.greatclips.com).

  3. 3

    Print the Page

    Press Ctrl+P (Windows) or Cmd+P (Mac) to open the print dialog. Select your printer and print.

  4. 4

    Bring to Great Clips

    Show your printed coupon to the stylist before your haircut begins.

Printable Coupon FAQ

Do I have to print the coupon?

No! Great Clips accepts digital coupons shown on your phone. Printing is optional.

Can I print in black and white?

Yes, black and white printouts are accepted. Color is not required.

Can I print multiple copies?

You can print multiple copies, but remember it's still one coupon per person per visit. You can't use two coupons on the same haircut.

The coupon didn't print correctly. What do I do?

Just show the coupon on your phone instead. Or try taking a screenshot and printing that.

Can I email the coupon to myself?

Yes! Open the coupon on your computer, then email yourself the link. Open it on your phone at the salon.
