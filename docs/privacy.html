Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Overview

GreatClipsDeal.com ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you visit our website at greatclipsdeal.com (the "Site").

The short version: We collect minimal data, don't sell your information, and use standard analytics to improve our site. That's it.

Information We Collect

  • Email Address: If you sign up for coupon alerts, we collect your email address.
  • Contact Form: If you contact us, we collect your name, email, and message.

Information Collected Automatically

  • Analytics Data: We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our site. This includes pages visited, time on site, and general location (city/country level).
  • Cookies: We use essential cookies to make the site work properly and analytics cookies to understand usage patterns.
  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, and screen size to ensure our site displays correctly.

How We Use Your Information

  • To send you coupon alerts (if you subscribed)
  • To respond to your inquiries
  • To improve our website and user experience
  • To analyze site traffic and usage patterns
  • To prevent fraud and abuse

Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information only in these limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: We use third-party services like Google Analytics, Formspree (contact forms), and email services. These providers have their own privacy policies.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights.

Cookies

We use the following types of cookies:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for the site to function (e.g., remembering if you've seen a coupon)
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors use our site (Google Analytics)

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but some features may not work properly.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Analytics: Website analytics - Privacy Policy
  • Formspree: Contact form processing - Privacy Policy
  • GitHub Pages: Website hosting - Privacy Policy

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we have about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information
  • Unsubscribe from email communications
  • Opt out of analytics tracking

To exercise these rights, contact us.

Email Communications

If you subscribe to our email list, you can unsubscribe at any time by clicking the "unsubscribe" link in any email or by contacting us.

Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your information. Our site uses HTTPS encryption for all connections. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

Our Site is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us.

Changes to This Policy
