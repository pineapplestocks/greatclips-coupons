Frequently Asked Questions

How do I use a Great Clips coupon?

Show your coupon to the stylist before your haircut. You can display it on your phone or print it out. The discount will be applied at checkout.

Can I use multiple Great Clips coupons?

No, Great Clips only allows one coupon per customer per visit. Choose the coupon with the best discount for your visit.

Do Great Clips coupons work at any location?

Some coupons work at any US location (we mark these as "Best Value" on our site), while others are location-specific. Always check the coupon details.

How long are Great Clips coupons valid?

Most Great Clips coupons are valid for 14 days after you access them. The expiration date is shown on each coupon.

Are these coupons legitimate?

Yes! All our coupons are sourced from official Great Clips Facebook advertisements and link directly to offers.greatclips.com.

What if my coupon doesn't work?

Make sure the coupon hasn't expired and is valid for your location. If issues persist, the salon staff can help verify the offer.
