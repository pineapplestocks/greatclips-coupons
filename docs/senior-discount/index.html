Senior Discount FAQs

Does Great Clips have a senior discount?

Yes, Great Clips offers a senior discount for customers 65 and older. The discount is typically $2-3 off the regular adult haircut price, making senior haircuts around $13-17.

Can I use a coupon instead of the senior discount?

Yes! In fact, coupons usually save you MORE than the senior discount. A $5.99 coupon is cheaper than the $13-17 senior price. You can't stack both, but choose whichever saves more.

Is the senior discount the same at all locations?

Not always. Great Clips salons are franchises, so individual owners may set different senior discount amounts. Most offer $2-3 off.

What age qualifies for senior discount?

Most Great Clips locations offer the senior discount to customers 65 years and older.
